Resources

Here are many of the resources that I used to start and make my own homebrew campaign!

Websites:

The Angry GM

The Angry GM is a website dedicated to providing in-depth, no-nonsense advice for Game Masters, particularly for Dungeons & Dragons. Known for its blunt and humorous style, the site covers topics like campaign management, encounter design, and game mechanics in a detailed, often theory-heavy way. It features sections on hacking game rules, answering GM-related questions, and sharing real campaign experiences. The site also offers homebrew content, book recommendations, and paid content for supporters. If you're looking for deep, strategic GM insights with a sharp edge, this site is a valuable resource.

Red Ragged Fiend

Red Ragged Fiend is a blog dedicated to tabletop role-playing games (TTRPGs), offering insights and resources for both game masters (GMs) and players. The site features articles on worldbuilding, game mechanics, and campaign management, with a focus on systems like Dungeons & Dragons. Recent posts include topics such as answering common questions for new Dungeon Masters, exploring worldbuilding biomes through hex mapping, and crafting infinite dungeons. The author shares experiences from various RPG systems, including D&D, Fate, and Pathfinder, aiming to enhance the TTRPG experience for the community

D&D Beyond

D&D Beyond (dndbeyond.com) is the official digital toolset for Dungeons & Dragons, providing players and Dungeon Masters with online character sheets, rulebooks, and campaign management tools. It features an integrated character builder, interactive digital dice rolling, homebrew content creation, and a searchable rules compendium. Users can purchase official D&D books, access a vast library of monsters, spells, and magic items, and utilize tools for encounter building and combat tracking. The platform streamlines gameplay and is widely used for both in-person and online D&D sessions, making it a go-to resource for modern D&D players.

Books:

2024 Dungeon Master's Guide

The 2024 Dungeon Master's Guide is a revised and expanded edition designed to assist Dungeon Masters of all experience levels in creating engaging and immersive Dungeons & Dragons campaigns. This 384-page guide offers comprehensive tools and advice for crafting adventures, building worlds, and facilitating memorable gameplay experiences.

2024 Player's Handbook

The 2024 Player's Handbook is an updated and expanded guide for the fifth edition of Dungeons & Dragons, offering optimized rules for character creation, exploration, combat, equipment, spells, and more. This 384-page volume introduces an expanded selection of character origins, classes, and subclasses, all revised and balanced for enhanced gameplay. Notably, the term "race" has been replaced with "species" to promote inclusivity, and ability score adjustments are now tied to character backgrounds rather than species. The handbook also features a new Weapon Mastery system, providing weapon-focused characters with special properties for their melee weapons, thereby enhancing tactical options in combat. Visually, the book boasts striking illustrations and a streamlined design, making it both a comprehensive and engaging resource for players

Mordenkainen Presents: Monsters of the Multiverse

Mordenkainen Presents: Monsters of the Multiverse is a Dungeons & Dragons sourcebook that compiles and updates content from previous supplements, including Volo’s Guide to Monsters and Mordenkainen’s Tome of Foes. It features over 250 revised monster stat blocks and more than 30 playable species, designed for greater flexibility across different campaign settings. The book streamlines spellcaster stat blocks, rebalances monster challenge ratings, and removes setting-specific lore to create a more versatile resource. Playable species now follow a standardized format, allowing for more customization in character creation. With its expanded bestiary and refined mechanics, the book serves as a valuable tool for Dungeon Masters and players looking to enhance their D&D experience.

2014 Monster Manual

The 2014 Monster Manual is a core rulebook for Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition, providing Dungeon Masters with a vast collection of creatures to populate their worlds. It contains over 400 monster stat blocks, ranging from classic fantasy creatures like dragons and beholders to lesser-known foes from D&D lore. Each entry includes detailed descriptions, lore, and unique abilities to help DMs craft engaging encounters. The book also features stunning artwork and streamlined mechanics that align with 5e’s simplified yet tactical gameplay. As one of the three core rulebooks, alongside the Player’s Handbook and Dungeon Master’s Guide, the Monster Manual is an essential resource for creating rich and dynamic adventures.
